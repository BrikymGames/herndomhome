Community

Public Programs

Lectures & Seminars

Monthly talks featuring historians, scholars, and community leaders on African American enterprise, preservation, and Atlanta history.

Youth & Family Programs

Storytime in the garden, summer history camp, family craft days, and teen docent training programs.

Heritage Workshops

Community workshops on genealogy, oral history collection, and traditional crafts inspired by the home's artisans.

Internships

College and graduate students gain hands-on experience in museum operations, archives, education, and preservation.
