Group & School Visits

We welcome school groups, homeschool co-ops, senior centers, and community organizations. Group rates and customized programming available.

  • Aligned with Georgia social studies standards
  • Hands-on activities for K–12 students
  • Pre- and post-visit curriculum materials
  • Minimum 10 visitors for group rate ($8/person)

Parking

Free street parking is available on University Place and surrounding streets. Accessible parking is located directly in front of the museum entrance.

Public Transit

MARTA Bus Route 51 stops nearby. The Vine City and Ashby stations are a short ride or walk away.

Accessibility

Accessible Visits

The Herndon Home is committed to welcoming all visitors. The main floor is wheelchair accessible. Large-print guides and assistive listening devices are available at the front desk.
